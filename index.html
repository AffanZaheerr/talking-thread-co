Our Story

Made with Heart & Yarn

Talking Thread Co is a passionate handmade art studio based in Lucknow & NCR, India — specializing in crochet art, embroidery, and custom gifting.

From delicate crochet flowers and charming keychains to bespoke embroidery hoops — everything is handmade, one stitch at a time.

We believe handmade items carry a warmth that mass-produced pieces simply can't replicate. Manufacturing in Lucknow & Noida.
